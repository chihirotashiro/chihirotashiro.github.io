Project1
Sales Analysis

Business request and user stories

An executive sales report for sales managers was the requested business outcome for this data analyst project. User stories were established to complete delivery and guarantee that acceptance criteria were upheld throughout the project based on the request that was presented by the business.

The requests were as below.

  • Get a dashboard overview of internet sales.
  • A detailed overview of Internet Sales per Customers.
  • A detailed overview of Internet Sales per Products.
  • A dashboard overview of internet sales.

Data cleaning and transformation (SQL)

The following tables were retrieved using SQL to generate the appropriate data model for conducting analysis and meeting the business needs outlined in the user stories.

DIM_Calendar

DIM_Customers

DIM_Products

FACT_InternetSales

Data model

The data model after the cleaned and prepped tables were read into Power BI.

This data model also demonstrates the connections between FACT Budget, FACT InternetSales, and other essential DIM tables.

Sales management dashboard

One page serves as the dashboard and overview for the completed sales management dashboard, while the other two pages combine tables to provide the essential details and visualizations to show sales over time, by customers, and by items.
